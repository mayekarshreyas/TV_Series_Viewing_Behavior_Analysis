TV Series Viewing Behavior Analysis

Overview
This project analyzes TV series viewing behavior to understand audience preferences, popular genres, and viewing patterns.

Objectives
Analyze viewer behavior and watching habits
Identify popular TV series and genres
Understand audience engagement and viewing time

Dataset
Dataset includes 300 viewers with details such as:
Age, Gender, Country
Viewing Time and Hours Watched
Viewer Type and Ratings

Key Insights
18–25 age group dominates viewership
Sci-Fi is the most popular genre
Stranger Things is the most popular series
Most viewers watch at night
Fantasy has the highest ratings

Tools Used
Excel
Pivot Tables
Data Visualization

Conclusion
The analysis helps understand viewer preferences, enabling better content planning and audience targeting.
